Negotiate Like You Know What You're Worth

Most people leave money on the table not because they don't deserve more — but because they don't know what to say, or freeze when it gets uncomfortable. RISN gives you the full toolkit: the email, the verbal script, the silence strategy, and the emotional coaching for the moment your hands start shaking.

📧 Personal negotiation email

Built from your specific accomplishments and situation — not a template anyone could send.

📞 Verbal script + silence strategy

What to say, when to stop talking, and exactly what to do when they go quiet — which is the moment most people cave.

🧠 Emotional coaching

Negotiation is psychological. RISN acknowledges the fear and gives you the one thing to hold onto when it gets uncomfortable.

🔄 Counter-offer + acceptance

When they come back lower — and they often do — you'll know exactly what to say to keep moving toward yes.
