Negotiate Like You Know What You're Worth

Most people leave money on the table not because they don't deserve more — but because they don't know what to say. RISN gives you the negotiation email, the counter-offer response, and the verbal script for when it gets awkward.

📧 The negotiation email

Professional, specific, and confident. Makes the ask in a way that opens dialogue rather than closing it.

🔄 Counter-offer response

What to say when they come back lower than you asked — keeps you in the conversation and moving toward yes.

📞 Verbal script

Word-for-word guidance for the phone call version — including exactly what to say after they go quiet.

✅ Acceptance email

When you reach a number you can live with — a professional close that sets the right tone going forward.

💡 The goal isn't to win an argument. It's to reach an agreement both sides feel good about. RISN keeps the tone collaborative throughout.
