Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2025 · Effective immediately

We believe privacy policies should be readable. This one is written in plain English. If you have questions, email us at risewithrisn@gmail.com.

1. Who we are

RISN ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website getrisn.com and provides AI-powered career tools including a resume builder, cover letter generator, interview coach, and company research tool. Our contact email is risewithrisn@gmail.com.

2. What information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Email address when you redeem a promo code or make a purchase — used only to validate and track your session access
  • Payment information processed securely by Stripe (we never store your card details)
  • Career information you enter into our tools — work history, skills, education, job descriptions
  • Company names you research using our company intel tool
  • Communications you send us

We also collect information automatically when you use our service:

  • Log data including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent
  • Device information including hardware model and operating system
  • Usage data about how you interact with our tools
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 7)

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our AI career tools
  • Process your payments and send you receipts
  • Send you service-related communications if you have opted in
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our service
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

4. How we share your information

We share your information only with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate RISN:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data. Visit stripe.com/privacy.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — powers our AI tools. Content you enter into our tools is processed by Anthropic's API. Visit anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Vercel — website hosting and infrastructure.
  • Google Analytics — anonymous website traffic analysis.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of RISN and its users.

5. Data retention

We retain your email address only as long as needed to validate your active session access. Career content you generate using our tools (resumes, cover letters, interview questions, etc.) is processed in real time and not stored on our servers after your session ends. You may request deletion of any data we hold by emailing us at risewithrisn@gmail.com.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to how we process your data
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email us at risewithrisn@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, to delete it, and to opt out of its sale (we do not sell personal information). To make a request, email us at the address above.

European residents (GDPR): Our legal basis for processing your data is contract performance (to provide our services), legitimate interests (to improve our product and prevent fraud), and consent where applicable. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our website and analyze traffic. Specifically:

  • Essential cookies — necessary for the website to function
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics, used to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymous and aggregated.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our service.

8. Data security

We take security seriously. We use industry-standard measures to protect your data including HTTPS encryption, secure payment processing through Stripe (PCI DSS compliant), and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's privacy

RISN is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. International data transfers

RISN is operated from the United States. If you access our service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. By using RISN, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of RISN after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
