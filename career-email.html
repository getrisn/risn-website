The Emails That Get You Hired

Most candidates nail the interview then disappear into the void. The ones who actually get hired communicate differently — before the call, after every conversation, and all the way to the offer. RISN writes all four emails for you.

Pre-Interview Intro

Send 24-48 hours before your interview

Set the tone before you say a word. Candidates who reach out first consistently report warmer, more collaborative interviews.

Post-Recruiter Follow-Up

Send within 24 hours of a recruiter call

The recruiter is your advocate — or your gatekeeper. A thoughtful follow-up keeps you human in a sea of résumés.

Post-Interview Thank You

Send within 2 hours of any interview

Generic thank-you emails go nowhere. Reference something specific from the conversation and remind them why you're the one.

Post-Final Round Close

Send after your last interview round

Close the deal. A confident, specific close email after your final round keeps you top of mind while they deliberate.
